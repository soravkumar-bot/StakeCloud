✦ Our Company

We Are StakeCloud

Building cloud infrastructure that unlocks the potential of emerging digital economies — one market at a time.

🎯

Our Vision

A world where every enterprise — regardless of geography — has access to the same caliber of cloud infrastructure, data intelligence, and digital tools as Silicon Valley's top companies.

We envision StakeCloud as the backbone of digital transformation across the post-Soviet and Central Asian sphere, enabling a generation of businesses to compete on the global stage.

🚀

Our Mission

To deliver enterprise-grade cloud and analytics solutions that are compliant, reliable, and genuinely affordable for businesses operating in Eastern Europe and Central Asia.

We achieve this through relentless investment in regional infrastructure, deep regulatory expertise, and a support model that speaks your language — literally and figuratively.

✦ The Journey

Our founding story

StakeCloud was founded in 2018 in Tbilisi, Georgia, by a team of engineers who had spent years watching world-class technology remain inaccessible to businesses in their region. The tools existed — but the pricing, compliance, and support structures were all built for Western markets.

Our founders — veterans from Google Cloud, Amazon, and regional fintech leaders — came together with a clear mandate: build infrastructure that is genuinely designed for the markets it serves.

We started with three clients in Georgia. By the end of our first year, we had expanded to five countries. Today, we operate across 12 nations, serving over 850 enterprise clients ranging from fintech startups to government agencies.

What hasn't changed is our core belief: technology should be an equalizer. StakeCloud exists to level the playing field, one data center at a time.
